Weight Loss Success Stories Get inspired by M.Al-Anazi and her amazing, real-life success story

My experience with starting a diet for the first time in my life was initially filled with apprehension. As soon as I consulted with the doctor and received my initial assessment along with a well-balanced diet plan, I found the first week to be quite comfortable and diverse. Contrary to what I had heard about the first week being the most challenging, I found myself losing a significant amount of weight, around two kilograms, within the first week. The doctor was always responsive, answering any questions and addressing any concerns I had, making adjustments to the plan whenever necessary.

As the weeks progressed, the second, third, and fourth weeks felt even easier, and my cravings for the foods that had previously caused my weight gain, such as rice, carbonated drinks, and cakes, diminished significantly. The changes in my measurements were noticeable, and everyone around me could see the difference, which motivated me to continue until I reach my goal.

With the grace of God, I am determined to keep going until I achieve my target weight. The diet plan has helped me modify my eating habits, breaking free from my previous indulgences like late-night snacks of chocolate with cola or chips. I now feel very comfortable with intermittent fasting.

Lastly, I am grateful to the doctor for several reasons. Firstly, I had planned to start my diet on January 8th, like many others who often postpone it, but he encouraged me and reminded me that the best time to start is now. Thanks to his guidance, I have made significant progress, achieving more than half of my goal by that date.
